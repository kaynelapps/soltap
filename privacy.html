Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data: tip jar configurations stored locally in your browser. We don't store wallet addresses or transaction data on our servers.

2. How We Use Data

Local storage is used only to remember your tip jar settings. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Solana blockchain for transactions
  • CDN services for libraries
  • GitHub Pages for hosting

4. Your Rights

You can clear your browser data anytime to remove all stored information. We don't have access to your wallet or funds.

5. Updates

This policy may be updated. Check this page periodically for changes.
